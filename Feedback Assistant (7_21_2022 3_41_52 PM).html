Description
Please describe the issue and what steps we can take to reproduce it:
When connected to a Magic Keyboard, Ctrl + ⌘ + Q should cause iPhone to lock. As far as I know, it should respond identically to a single press of the Sleep/Wake button. I have been unable to determine how to reproduce this issue - **I have zero insight on when/why responsiveness ceases due to to how infrequently I need to lock my device.** As per my experiences in the past few days, however, of being conscious of the error and therefore attempting to reproduce it, I can say definitively that it is happening quite often. (Yes, I *have* tested my Magic Keyboard to verify it is functioning correctly.)
